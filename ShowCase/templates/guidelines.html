On the journey of thoughts

GUIDELINES

1. Read all the guidelines

2. Provide Feedback for anything you think can be improved upon. We are in process of making ThirdDime experience complete. Your feedback will greatly help in the process

Interpretations

1. While writing an interpretation or story, try to Contribute to the discussion on the artwork. Feel free to write any opinion on the artwork as long as it contributes to the context of the artwork. If you have nothing to contribute but want to show your appreciation for the artwork, then upvote the interpretation or story which relates best to the artwork in your opinion. Add comments to add to the discussion.

2. Interpretations that don't add to the conversation may be subject to removal.

3. If you want to critique an artwork , provide constructive criticism.

4. Feel free to Submit your own opinion on the artwork, even if an interpretation already exists on similar lines.

5. Do not leave links to pages selling art.

6. Use proper spelling and grammar. Do not write in all CAPS.

7. Report any Spam you find.

Artworks

1. Give Credit to the artist. Always make sure to give credit to the right artist and ensure proper permissions before submitting an artwork.

2. Give the Correct title of the artwork without any extra information. You can add the additional information in description.

3. Moderators reserve the right to remove submissions, that aren't high quality.

4. Do not submit sketches, doodles or unfinished work.

5. Report any spam you find. Report artworks which are not credited to the right artist.
